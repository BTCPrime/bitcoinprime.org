About

Primecoin is a unique blockchain with innovative proof-of-work based on prime numbers and is the first cryptocurrency in the world where the algorithms that secure the blockchain also create mathematically useful and interesting by-products. Primecoin shows that the computational effort used in proof-of-work can have a multitude of real world applications.

Bitcoin Prime is the cryptocurrency platform that will be created from the upcoming fork of Primecoin and Bitcoin. Bitcoin Prime is also a team of people that are passionate about useful proof-of-work and solving the myriad of problems that blockchains face today; from vulnerabilities in regard to 51% attacks to scaling issues. Expanding and supporting Primecoin is our first step on this journey.

Inspired by Primecoin’s unique POW, we aim to bring this undervalued coin up-to-date and to expand its capabilities to solve modern problems in crypto. If you are interested in participating in this exploration with us please join the Discord.

Hard Fork

Bitcoin Prime will be a fork-merge of Primecoin and Bitcoin. It will support the Lightning network and implement Segwit. Bitcoin Prime will retain Primecoin’s unique proof-of-work where the results of the computational power of its miners are mathematically significant. It will also have 1 min blocks, making payment transactions 10x faster than the bitcoin network.

The fork date is yet to be announced. Please stay tuned to all of our our public channels for the most up-to-date information (this website, Twitter, our Telegram, subreddit, and our Discord).

What is Primecoin?
Primecoin is a unique blockchain with innovative Proof-of-Work based on prime numbers and is the first cryptocurrency in the world where the algorithms that secure the blockchain also create mathematically useful and interesting by-products. Primecoin shows that the computational effort used in Proof-of-Work can have a multitude of real world applications. Primecoin also processes payment transactions 10x faster than the bitcoin network with its 1 minute blocks. You can learn more about Primecoin here: primecoin.io
What is useful proof-of-work?
Useful proof-of-work seeks to put the vast computational efforts of miners to the best possible use. The bitcoin network currently performs about 34 million tera hashes per second and uses an estimated 68.53 TWh per year of electricity. This can be equated to the energetic power of about 52 of The Large Hadron Collider particle accelerators, the largest machine in the world! The growth of computing power used in cryptocurrency networks has increased exponentially. Sadly, the mathematical results of these efforts are largely useless after each block has been created. With Primecoin the results of these computational efforts have value in themselves.
How can I receive Bitcoin Prime?
To participate in the fork and receive your Bitcoin Prime you’ll need to hold your Primecoin and/or Bitcoin in a supported wallet where you control your own private keys. For Primecoin this is the paper wallet, full-node wallet, or the NEW Electrum wallet (In Testing). We are also in contact with Coinomi and hope to have the Bitcoin Prime fork supported there as well.

We have a lot of exciting plans for how Primecoin’s unique Proof-of-Work can be expanded to solve current issues in cryptocurrency scaling and usability. If you are interested in participating in this exploration with us please join the Discord. Once the white paper is ready, it will be released via our public channels.
When will you announce the fork date for Bitcoin Prime?
We have several foundational development tasks that must be accomplished before a fork date can be released. When we have completed them and are certain we can meet a specific fork date, we will announce that date to our community via our public channels (this website, Twitter, our Telegram, subreddit, and our Discord).
